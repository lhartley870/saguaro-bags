Cactus Leather - Our Ethical Philosophy

At Saguaro Bags we care about saving the planet. The cactus leather used to make our vegan leather bags is an organic and partially biodegradable material that is helping to reduce the plastic pollution crisis. It's a viable alternative to both animal-derived leather and traditional vegan leathers. Animal leathers emit toxic chemicals and have obvious animal cruelty implications. Traditional vegan leathers are made from highly toxic PVC or polyurethane. We are excited to be part of the sustainable movement taking fashion into the future. If you are interested in leading a more sustainable life, check out the Guardian's article 50 Simple Ways to Make Your Life Greener.

Affordable Vegan Bags - Ethical Style

Plastic-based vegan leather alternatives can be very affordable but look cheap whilst more sustainable options like apple leather, wood leather and cactus leather can be more expensive than animal leather. Here at Saguaro Bags we believe that choosing sustainable leather bags should be a more affordable option than animal based leather and that you should not have to compromise on style or fashion to follow your ethics. Our affordable vegan handbags are priced to be competitive, are designed to be classic yet stylish and will last up to 10 years, giving you investment pieces you can enjoy for years to come.

The Technology - How Cactus Leather is Made

Desserto cactus leather are leading the way in the manufacture of cactus leather. Cactus is the most abundant plant in Mexico and doesn't need a lot of water to grow, unlike the cows raised for animal leather. Desserto leather is made from the leaves of the nopal cactus and they don't even kill their cacti to make cactus leather! They just remove the mature leaves, leaving the smaller ones to grow, making it even more ethical! The leaves are cut, cleaned, mashed, dried for 3 days, mixed with non-toxic chemicals and made into any texture or colour you want! To find out more check out Desserto Leather's website.
